The Mofflys

The Mofflys is a vr shooter game that will be released on playstation, steam, oculus and more in 2019. In the game you are a creation of the mofflys, 'murder clone'. With a wide variety of weapons you will play through sets of minigames to invade earth and cause mayhem. The game exists of multiple worlds with classic themes like Egypt, Amazon River, Western world and more.

Space Escape is a 3D platformer which is based around the element challenge. In this game you are trapped in space. And you have to find your way through the asteroids by collecting currency with which you can purchase powerups at the vendor machine.

Orbit Singularity is an orbit game created during the 2018 Global Gamejam. You try to find extraterrestial life by sending signals through as many satellites as possible.
